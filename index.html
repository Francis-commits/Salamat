A Letter to the Souls I've Met

2024 — 2025 & BEYOND

I was thinking tonight about how many people it takes to build a single person.

I am made of the advice you gave me when I was lost, the inside jokes we laughed about until we couldn't breathe, and even the quiet moments where we didn't say anything at all, but I knew you were there.

To those who stayed: You are my heart’s permanent residents. To those who left: Thank you for the chapter you wrote in my book. I still read it sometimes.

You may think you were just a "stranger" or a "classmate," but to me, you were a witness to my life. And for that, I love you.

Merry Christmas & Thank you.

The Quiet Supporters

To the people who liked my posts, checked in when I went silent, or just sent a "thinking of you" text. You have no idea how many times you saved a bad day without even knowing it.

The Brief Encounters

To the person in the grocery line, the waiter who was extra kind, the stranger who complimented my shirt—you were only in my life for 60 seconds, but you reminded me that the world is a gentle place.

The Lessons

To the people I no longer talk to: I don't hold any bitterness. You taught me how to set boundaries, how to love better, or simply how to let go. You are still a part of my growth.

The Life-Longs

To those who have seen me change three different versions of myself and still stayed. You are my family, whether by blood or by choice. Thank you for the years of patience.

I AM WHO I AM BECAUSE OF YOU.

DECEMBER 2025
